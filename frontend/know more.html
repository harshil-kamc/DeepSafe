Forensic Methodology

DeepSafe utilizes a multi-layered verification process to determine the authenticity of digital media. Unlike standard filters, our system inspects the hidden data structures and pixel-level inconsistencies that AI generators often leave behind.

Detection Vectors

  • Metadata Fingerprinting: Scanning EXIF, XMP, and IPTC headers for hardware signatures vs. AI software "stamps."
  • Frequency Analysis: Detecting "checkerboard artifacts" in the high-frequency spectrum invisible to the human eye.
  • Biological Consistency: Analyzing skin texture and eye reflections for organic randomness.

Understanding Results

0-35% LEGITIMATE: Standard capture patterns and valid hardware metadata.
36-70% SUSPICIOUS: Significant edits or AI-upscaling detected.
71-100% AI GENERATED: Synthetic origin confirmed via neural artifacts.

Security Best Practices

Never Trust, Always Verify: Do not click embedded links if flagged suspicious.

Check the Source: Verify sender addresses for AI-generated phishing.
